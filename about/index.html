History

2016

The Graphics Room was originally founded in 2016 by Johan Benevidas as a side project. The idea came from the room name him and his design team worked in at his former employer.

2019

By 2019, Johan was able to acquire reoccurring customers, such as business like Mr East, Central Park, The Hub, Teak, Nikki’s, and Zio Toto. At the same time, he was able to turn this freelance side business into a legitimate LLC.
